  About Me

I'm a Software Engineering graduate from SMKN 3 Palu. I interned as a Web Developer at PT. Wesclic Indonesia Neotech, a digital transformation company in Yogyakarta, where I shipped production-ready full-stack applications end-to-end, covering everything from database schema and REST API design to frontend UI and deployment, working primarily with Laravel, Livewire, Alpine.js, and MySQL on the PHP side, and React, Next.js, TypeScript, Express.js, and PostgreSQL on the modern JS stack. I'm comfortable across the full lifecycle: requirements, architecture, coding, testing, and shipping.

Outside of web, I build across platforms too: Android AR apps in C# and Unity, desktop GUI tools in Python, and anything that requires solving a non-trivial problem. In October 2025, my AR climate-education app ClimaGlobe won 4th Place at a national-level competition hosted by Universitas Tadulako. I also hold a TOEIC score of 940, placing me at full professional working proficiency in English. I pick up new stacks quickly, work well in team environments, and care about shipping things that actually work.
